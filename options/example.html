POSSESSION OF LIGHTSABER

(Generally)

(1) A lightsaber may not be possessed by any person unless the person:

(a) Is a Jedi, as defined in 980.100, 980.730 or 985.500; or

(b) Is licensed as a lightsaber transporter or reseller under 989.005 to 989.100.

(2) A violation of this section is a class Q felony.

[Formerly 980.200; 22 c.1133 §3; 30 c.424 §399]

Note: 989.005 was enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but was not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 989 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation.

(Licensing)

[Formerly 980.780; repealed by 72 c.1133 §8]
